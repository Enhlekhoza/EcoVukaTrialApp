How It Works

1. Organizing

Before requesting collection, recyclable waste needs to be cleaned, separated, and packaged in the correct way to facilitate the collector's work.

2. Scheduling

After separating the materials, it's time to open EcoVuka and look for the waste picker closest to your location. Arrange the best day and time for collection.

3. Collection

When meeting with the collector, don't forget to reward them for their service. Recycling is a daily challenge that deserves recognition.

4. Impact

Finishing a collection through EcoVuka contributes to the environment and increases the collectors' income. Value and remunerate the work of those who make recycling happen!
